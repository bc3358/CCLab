World War Ducks

In the year 2026, two groups of rubber ducks go head to head in a heated battle for total domination. World War Ducks turns a harmless childhood toy into the center of a chaotic global conflict, mixing humor, action, and absurdity. Even though the project is playful and entertaining, it also reflects how rivalry and division can spiral into something much bigger. By using rubber ducks, I wanted to create something visually fun while still hinting at the idea that even silly conflicts can mirror real-world tension and destruction
